Metal 3D Printing Services

01

DMLS Production

Direct Metal Laser Sintering at 20-60μm layer thickness, delivering 99.5% density parts in Ti6Al4V, Inconel 718, CoCr, 17-4 PH, 316L, and AlSi10Mg.

02

Alloy Selection

Expert guidance matching your application to the optimal alloy: 268 MPa aluminum for lightweighting, 1,365 MPa hardened steel for tooling, 993 MPa titanium for aerospace.

03

Post-Processing

HIP at 2,000°F + 15,000 psi, stress relief, CNC finishing to ±0.001", and CMM dimensional verification for production-ready parts.
